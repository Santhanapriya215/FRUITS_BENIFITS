Health Benefits of fruits

Apples
    Benefits

  • Heart health: Apples can help protect against heart damage and reduce the risk of heart disease. They contain soluble fiber and antioxidants, which may help lower blood pressure and cholesterol.
  • Digestion: Apples are high in fiber, which can help with digestion, diarrhea, and constipation. They also contain pectin, a type of fiber that acts as a prebiotic in your gut.
Grapes
    Benefits

  • Immune system: Vitamin C in grapes helps your immune system fight infections.
  • Heart health: Grapes contain potassium, which can help with heart health, and polyphenols, which can help prevent cancer and maintain heart health.
Guava
    Benefits

  • Digestion: Guava's high fiber content can help with constipation and diarrhea
  • Immune system: Guava is a rich source of vitamin C, which can help reduce the length of a cold and fight bacteria.
  • Blood sugar: Guava's antioxidants and fiber can help regulate blood sugar levels
  • Heart health: Guava's antioxidants can help protect the heart from damage.
  • Weight loss: Guava is low in calories and high in fiber, which can help you feel full and curb cravings.
  • Skin health: Guava's antioxidants can help protect your skin from oxidative damage
  • Menstrual cramps: Guava leaf extract may help relieve menstrual cramps
  • Cancer: Test tube and animal studies suggest that guava leaf extracts may inhibit or stop the growth of cancer cells.
Mango
    Benefits

  • Digestion: Mangoes contain both soluble and insoluble fiber, which can help with constipation and bloating. The fiber in mangoes can also promote regular bowel movements and support the growth of beneficial gut bacteria
  • Cholesterol: The soluble fiber in mangoes can help lower total cholesterol and LDL cholesterol.
Oranges
    Benefits

  • Vitamin C: Oranges are a great source of vitamin C, which can help protect cells from damage and boost your immune system. Vitamin C may also help your skin look smooth and supple
  • Heart health: Oranges contain potassium, which can help manage high blood pressure. They also contain antioxidants, which can reduce inflammation and cholesterol levels.
